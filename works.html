Some web games Just for fun

  • Sokoban: Start to play

    The game is played on a board of squares, where each square is a floor or a wall. Some floor squares contain boxes, and some floor squares are marked as storage locations.

    The player is confined to the board, and may move horizontally or vertically onto empty squares (never through walls or boxes). The player can also move into a box, which pushes it into the square beyond. Boxes may not be pushed into other boxes or walls, and they cannot be pulled. The puzzle is solved when all boxes are at storage locations.

starting from the database design as follow:

Home page

the Home page display all items (books) that are available for sell.

User registration

The registration page required user to enter their personal information, shipping address and billing address.

Product list

This page lists all the items that are available for user to select and purchase. Every row contains a detail link that leads the user to the product detail page.

Product detail

This page shows the detail description of the product that was selected. When the user login to their account, they are allowed to select quantity they want to purchase and add it to their shopping cart.

Shopping cart

Once the user click on their shopping cart page, thet system will load their selected while unpaid items from the database. They will see what they have in the cart and how much they have to pay for. Beside, they can remove the items from the cart if thet want to.
There is a check out button on the top left conner that leads them to the payment page.
